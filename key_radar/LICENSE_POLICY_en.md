## Activation and device management
- License keys are activated by the user from within the KeyRadar app or via the Polar Customer Portal.
- If the activation limit (2) is reached and a new device is needed, the user can deactivate one of the existing devices directly through the **Polar Customer Portal**. No operator approval required.
- Deactivation takes effect immediately; paid features on the deactivated device will be disabled at its next validation.

## License-key identity
- A license key is an asset issued to a *single purchaser* at the time of purchase.
- Sharing, transferring, or reselling to third parties violates §4.5 of the Terms and is grounds for revocation.

## Validation
- KeyRadar verifies license validity against Polar's validation endpoint at app startup and periodically.
- If a license is revoked (refund, cancellation, expiry, etc.), paid features will be disabled at the next validation.

## Update entitlement
- A one-time purchase includes update entitlement for **2 years**.
- After the update period ends, the features available at purchase time remain usable.
- To receive further updates after the update period, a new license purchase or a separate renewal policy may apply.

## Relationship with the free trial
- The free trial is separate from the license. See [Free Trial Policy](./TRIAL_POLICY_en.md).

## Monthly subscription (alternative option)
A monthly subscription may also be offered as an alternative pricing option, at USD $3.99/month if enabled. A monthly subscription grants access to paid features while the subscription remains active. If the subscription is canceled, expires, or payment fails, paid features may be disabled. Subscription cancellation stops future billing but does not automatically refund previous charges unless required by law or approved by support.
