## 2. Activation

On the Mac App Store, licenses are automatically activated via Apple In-App Purchase (StoreKit 2). No license key entry is required.

## 3. Device Count

Up to *2 devices* per Apple ID can be activated simultaneously. Activating a third device automatically deactivates the oldest one.

## 4. Transfer

Lifetime licenses are managed at the *Apple ID level*. Transfers between Apple IDs follow Apple's policy; KeyRadar does not provide a separate transfer process.

## 5. Refunds

Refunds follow Apple App Store policies. KeyRadar does not provide a separate refund process.
